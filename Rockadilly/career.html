Restaurant Manager

The person with this designation has a lot of responsibilities.So, for applying to this position you should have enough strength to handle the responsibilities.For this position, the qualifications are basics such as people skills and organisation.The candidates who want to apply must have a degree in business or hospitality.

Bartender

It is the top postion of the front house staff.His responibilities are for the service bar and for the customers who choose to eat at the bar.

Dishwasher

It is the most underrated position in the whole restaurant.Inspite of washing dishes, his other duties are cleaning floor, bathrooms and other maintainance tasks.Not many people like this job.But, if there will be no dishwasher in the restaurant, then the restaurant may not run.So, it is also one of the important jobs.For this position, no experience is required.

Host
